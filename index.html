Coming from Bangkok (5 hours of bus)

  • Go to Mo Chit 2 bus terminal
  • On the 2nd floor, there are a lot of cashiers where you can buy tickets (on the right side of this photo). Go to the ones on the left and ask for a ticket to Nang Rong. It should cost you less than 300 THB. There are buses every hour. If at a cashier they tell you that there is no bus soon, or the price is more than 300 THB, go to the next cashier and ask again until you find a bus leaving in the incoming hour.
  • Contact us (see contact page) if you can when your bus leaves so that we know more or less when you'll arrive.
  • Once you'll arrive in Nang Rong, you'll be most likely left at the bus terminal. If not, you'll be left in front of the hospital.
  • Get to the bus station if you are not there with a motorbike taxi, then take a shuttle to Ban Nong Ta-Kian, on the way to Lam Pai Mat.
    There are shuttles all day until 4-5pm, it cost about 30 THB, after that you'll have to take a taxi which is about 300 THB.
